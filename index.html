Frequently asked questions

Answer the questions that affect trust.

What should I say about supplement benefits?

Use careful wellness language like support, balance, or daily routine benefits. Avoid claiming that a product diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents disease unless you have the proper legal and scientific basis.

Should I include a disclaimer?

Yes. A visible wellness disclaimer improves clarity and helps frame the site responsibly.
